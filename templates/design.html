Concept & Design

Concept

Concept phase is the phase where you decide which services will be implemented, how and by whom, and finally what standards are you going to put in place.

It might apply to the whole property or to departments of it, such as F&B, Spa, Leisure, etc…

Concepts have to be inline with the property's level and fit in a global idea of the project.

Design

The purpose of the design phase is to match the construction with the services provided. It is a deep study of the workflows and environment in order to achieve the best customer's experience.

It can be focused on decoration, construction, or a resetting of the floor plan to reach a smooth operation, adding value to your product.
